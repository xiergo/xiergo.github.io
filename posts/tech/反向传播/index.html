反向传播

创建: 2023-01-20 | 更新: 2023-01-20 | 字数: 336字 | 时长: 1分钟 |  作者: Xiergo  | 标签:    | 访问:

$$ \pmb a^{(1)}=f(\pmb x^{(1)})=f(\pmb W^{(1)}\pmb x^{(0)}) $$

$$ \pmb a^{(k)}=f(\pmb x^{(k)})=f(\pmb W^{(k)} \pmb a^{(k-1)})=\text {sigmoid}(\pmb{W}^{(k)}\pmb{a}^{(k-1)}) \\k=2,3,…,K $$

$$ \pmb y=\pmb a^{(K)}=f(\pmb x^{(K)}) $$

第k层的i节点的输出值 $$ a_i^{(k)}=\text{sigmoid}(\sum_j w_{ij}^{(k)} a_j^{(k-1)}) $$ 对于输出节点$i$，误差项$\delta_i$如下： $$ \begin{aligned} \delta_i&=-\frac{\partial \ell}{\partial x_i}\\ &=-\frac{\partial}{\partial x_i}(\frac{1}{2}(t_i-y_i)^2)\\ &=\frac{\partial y_i}{\partial x_i}(t_i-y_i)\\ &=\frac{\partial}{\partial x_i} (\frac{1}{1+e^{-x_i}})(t_i-y_i)\\ &=\frac{e^{-x_i}}{(1+e^{-x_i})^2}(t_i-y_i)\\ &=y_i(1-y_i)(t_i-y_i) \end{aligned} $$ 对于最后一隐藏层的节点$i$，误差项$\delta_i$如下： $$ \begin{aligned} \delta_i&=-\frac{\partial \ell}{\partial x_i}\\ &=-\sum_{j\in \text{output}}\frac{\partial \ell}{\partial x_j}\frac{\partial x_j}{\partial a_i}\frac{\partial a_i}{\partial x_i}\\ &=a_i(1-a_i)\sum_{j\in \text{output}}w_{ji}\delta_j \end{aligned} $$ 更新权重： $$ \begin{aligned} w_{ij}’&=w_{ij}-\eta \frac{\partial \ell}{\partial w_{ij}}\\ &=w_{ij}-\eta\frac{\partial \ell}{\partial x_i}\frac{\partial x_i}{\partial w_{ij}}\\ &=w_{ij}+\eta \delta_i a_j \end{aligned} $$

神经网络

计算$x_4,x_5,x_6,x_7$： $$ x_4=\sum_{j\in {1,2,3}} w_{4j}x_j $$ 计算$a_4,a_5,a_6,a_7$： $$ a_4=\text{sigmoid}(x_4)=\frac{1}{1+e^{-x_4}} $$ 计算$x_8,x_9$ $$ x_8=\sum_{j\in {4,5,6,7}} w_{8j}a_j $$ 计算$y_8,y_9$ $$ y_8=\text{sigmoid}(x_8) $$ 计算$\delta_8,\delta_9$ $$ \delta_8=y_8(1-y_8)(t_8-y_8) $$ 计算$\delta_4,\delta_5,\delta_6,\delta_7$ $$ \delta_4=a_4(1-a_4)\sum_{j\in{8,9}} w_{j4}\delta_j $$ 更新所有$w_{ij}$ $$ w_{ij}’=w_{ij}+\eta\delta_ia_j $$

$$ w_{81}’=w_{81}+\eta\delta_8a_1 $$

$$ w_{41}’=w_{41}+\eta\delta_4 x_1 $$
